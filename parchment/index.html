The Seventeenth of February, MMXXVI

Founded by Costa Zinzopoulos, Endless Psychosis exists at the intersection of art and clothing. Each piece is crafted as a statement — a visual meditation on what endures beyond the temporary. The brand draws upon the weight of history, the elegance of the Victorian era, and the raw honesty of the human condition to create garments that are not merely worn, but experienced.

Every collection is conceived as a chapter in an ongoing chronicle — a living archive that grows richer with each edition. We do not follow seasons. We follow obsessions.

“Everything must come to an end but we decide on what truly is endless.”
