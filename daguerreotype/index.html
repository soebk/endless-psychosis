Where Art
Meets Garment

Founded by Costa Zinzopoulos, Endless Psychosis exists at the intersection of art and clothing. Each piece is crafted as a statement — a visual meditation on what endures beyond the temporary. Drawing from Victorian aesthetics, darkened romanticism, and the raw poetry of the human condition, the brand transforms fabric into gallery-worthy expression.
